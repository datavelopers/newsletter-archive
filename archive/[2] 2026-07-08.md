# [DATAVELOPERS Digest #2 - July 8th, 2026]

**[💻 Open-Source]**

- [github.com/invergent-ai/surogate](http://github.com/invergent-ai/surogate) by [Flavius Burca](https://www.linkedin.com/in/flaviusburca) and the team at [Surogate](https://www.linkedin.com/company/surogate/) - Surogate Trainer is built for developers and enterprises that need fast experimentation, whether running on-premise or in the cloud.

- [github.com/tudorAndrei/criv](http://github.com/tudorAndrei/criv) by [Tudor Andrei Dumitrașcu](https://www.linkedin.com/in/tudor-pythia/) - Local CLI for keeping repository docs, ADRs, architecture notes, and source references connected as a validated knowledge graph.

- [github.com/ignatandrei/BlocklyAutomation](http://github.com/ignatandrei/BlocklyAutomation) by [Ignat Andrei](https://www.linkedin.com/in/ignatandrei/) - Blockly Automation is a tool for LowCode / automation of sites (Swagger / OpenAPI / RPA) and PC made of [Blockly](https://blockly.com/).

**[📚 Books]**

- [The PRFAQ Framework](https://www.amazon.com/PRFAQ-Framework-Adapting-Amazons-Innovation-ebook/dp/B0DPXT6YTV) by [Marcelo Calbucci](https://www.linkedin.com/in/marcelocalbucci/) - Presents the innovation method pioneered at Amazon and offers a practical way to apply the widely known "working backwards" approach.

**[📝 Articles/Essays]**

- [On Bullshit](https://www.csc2.ncsu.edu/faculty/mpsingh/local/Social/f24/wrap/readings/Harry-Frankfurt-On-Bullshit-1988.pdf) by Harry G. Frankfurt - First published as an essay in 1986 and later as a book in 2005, it offers a sharp theory of what bullshit actually is and how it works in communication.
