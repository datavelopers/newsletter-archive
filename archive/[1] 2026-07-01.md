# [DATAVELOPERS Digest #1 - July 1st, 2026]

[💻 Open-Source]

- [huggingface.co/univec](http://huggingface.co/univec) by [Adrian M](https://www.linkedin.com/in/adrian-m-420799409/) - Vector conversion models that map embeddings from one model's vector space into another without re-embedding the source text.

- [github.com/octokraft/agent-vault](http://github.com/octokraft/agent-vault) by [Ciprian Grigore](https://www.linkedin.com/in/cipriangrigore1/) - Zero-knowledge secret injection for AI agents. Agents reference secrets by name. The vault injects them into commands via environment variables or stdin pipes. There is no command to retrieve secrets in plaintext.

- [agentmarkup.dev](http://agentmarkup.dev/) & [github.com/agentmarkup/agentmarkup](http://github.com/agentmarkup/agentmarkup) by [Sebastian Cochinescu](https://www.linkedin.com/in/sebastiancochinescu/) - Build-time llms.txt, optional A2A Agent Cards, JSON-LD, markdown mirrors, AI crawler controls, Content-Signal headers, and validation for Next.js, Vite, and Astro websites.

[📃 Research Papers]

- [GLM-5.2: Built for Long-Horizon Tasks](https://paperswithcode.co/paper/98456) - [Z.ai](http://z.ai/)'s latest flagship open-weight model for long-horizon agentic engineering.

[📝 Articles]

- [The Twilight of AI Vampires](https://essays.cborodescu.com/p/the-twilight-of-ai-vampires) - Do AI coding agents genuinely enhance developers' cognitive abilities or do they merely increase output at the cost of cognitive degradation?

[📚 Books]

- [Automation and Utopia: Human Flourishing in a World without Work](https://www.amazon.com/Automation-Utopia-Human-Flourishing-without/dp/0674984242) by [John Danaher](https://www.linkedin.com/in/john-danaher-1935b81/) - Automation renders humans obsolete. Free from need or want, we can spend our time inventing and playing games and exploring virtual realities that are more deeply engaging and absorbing than any we have experienced before, allowing us to achieve idealized forms of human flourishing.

[🎙️ Podcasts]

- [AI And The Knowledge Economy on Thinking With Mitch Joel](https://www.sixpixels.com/podcast/archives/ai-and-the-knowledge-economy-with-sangeet-paul-choudary-twmj-1040/) - Why AI is more than a productivity tool: it changes what is scarce and, in turn, where value is created. Drawing on examples from Kodak, Netflix, cloud computing, platforms, and supply chains, [Mitch Joel](https://www.linkedin.com/in/mitchjoel/) and his guest, [Sangeet Paul Choudary](https://www.linkedin.com/in/sangeetpaul/), show why some organizations adapt while others remain trapped by the assumptions behind their past success.
